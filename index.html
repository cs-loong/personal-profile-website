Chassis Design and Manufacture
2D basic model was analaysed to obtain a reasonable range of torsional stiffness under 40-60 mass distribution (Oversteer setting). Analysis shows torsional stiffness should be about 4 to 6 times of front rolling stiffness. Tubular chassis frame underwent topology analysis on 5% to 15% of whole surface body in Ansys to obtain optimum triangulation structure and was constructed in Solidworks. The cockpit was designed after 3D scanning the ergonomics of our drivers on the ergonomic kit. The CAD Model was simulated in Ansys Structural to obtain torsional stiffness of about 1800N/deg Real world torsional testing was conducted and show 20% lower in torsional stiffness The chassis was built with 4130 Steel Alloy and underwent 3D scanning to check the misallignment with the CAD model. The car seat was manufactured with 8-layer of prepreg unidirectional carbon fibre. Half monocoque chassis was also designed but was not manufactured due to time constraint 3-point bending test and perimeter sheat test on carbon fibre composite with sandwitch structure
Aerodynamic System Design and Manufacture
Lap time simulation analysis to study effectiveness of aerodynamic packages to estimate the optimum L/D ratio. CAD Model was constructed with mainly surface modelling in Solidworks. CFD Simulation was conducted with Ansys Fluent to obtain Downforce and Dragforce as well as L/D ratio. To achieve lightweight and minimum cost, the airfoil was manufactured CFRP using resin infusion while the inner structure was made with balsa wood spars, aluminium plate and polycarbonate carbon fiber. Endplate is manufactured with sandwiching two 3 layers of CFRP with PET foam.
Accumulator Box Design and Manufacture
110 LiPo cells are connected in 110S1P (total 480V and 8kWhr) and seperated into 5 22S1P segments/sectors.
The battery cells were held together with silicon tape SCP-040 with high thermal conductivity for effective hear dissipation and then secured by FR4 (Fireproof material) plates to form a segment/sector.
The segments are designed to be seperated with steel plates insulated with Formex GK-10 (UL94 VTM-0 Rating).
Foolproof design: The maintenance socket/plug 3 different models such that no same socket models are allowed to be positioned next to each other or at the same sector. Only plug model with same key and keyway design are allowed to be plug into that specific socket. The high voltage wires are designed to have adequate length to avoid wrong plugins and crossing over.
Electrical Wire Hardness Manufacture
Wiring Diagram of the safety circuit of whole car. Prepare table to organise/easy-debug the sockets and corresponding plugs. Electrical wiring hardness manufacture with proper insulation with heat shrink tube.

Local Planning and Control
Local planning is conducted by interpolating the midpoints of cones from both sides which the coordinates of cones is obtained from perception node.
Control of the vehicle is achieved by pure pursuit algorithm by computing the angular velocity command that moves the robot from its current position to reach some look-ahead point in front of the vehicle.
Configure the logic planning for different mission such as Acceleration, Skid-pad, Autocross (Sprint) and Track Drive. The logic is tested in EUFS simulator.
Coding for local planning node and control node is constructed with python and run with ROS2 in Ubuntu 20.04.
Race Line Strategy
Reference: Braghin, Francesco, et al. "Race driver model." Computers & Structures 86.13-14 (2008): 1503-1516.
Center race line is discretized into many points, and treated as a “spline” so that the line will be continuous. - Knowing the inner and outer limit of the race, the new path generating is the ratio (α) of the point of inner track shifting towards outer track.
The shortest path and minimum curvature path can be formulated by setting up proper H matrixes and B matrixes and then calculated with quadratic programming to obtain the optimized value of the respective path
The velocity profile will be generated with four steps: (1) Velocity profile due to curvature, friction coefficient, and drag coefficient; (2) Maximum velocity constraint; (3) Feed forward Algorithm (constraint the velocity with maximum acceleration); (4) Feed backward Algorithm (constraint the velocity with maximum deceleration)
Final optimum race line will combine these two paths by assigning a weightage (e) to each path. By comparing the weightage from 0 to 1 to obtain the race line that takes lowest time.

Car Bumper Design Optimization
This project requires me to design a standalone bumper beam and crash box system at 60m/s and do crash analysis in CATIA V5 to test its crashworthiness. Further study is also conducted with different beam profiles to compare the Specific Energy Absorption (SEA) and the system's strength. However, it is noted that CATIA V5 is not a suitable tool for crash analysis, so static structural analysis with adequate force and acceleration (with a simplified crash pulse).
RedBull Front Wing CFD Simulation
I was tasked to finish CFD simulation on the given RedBull Front Wing. The simulation is required to be conducted in StarCCM+. With proper domain boundary that considered the ground effect, possible flow circulation error, wind-blocking errors, etc, the front wing is simulated under wind speed of 127km/h and 321.9km/h. The residual errors converged well and it appears to have low linearization error.
Non-premixed Flame Combustion on EGR System Simulation
Referring to the paper written by Branco, et al (2016), It was required to prove there is a reduction in NOx production with the EGR system. Three simulations with different compositions O2, N2 and CO2 as oxidizers (with increasing composition of CO2 and reduction of N2) are conducted to simulate the oxidizer in the EGR system. The fuel is the composition of methane and H2. The simulation is conducted in Ansys Fluent.
